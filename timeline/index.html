Timeline Project

by Isaac Fleetwood

      Before continuing though, you must know how to operate the controls. A mouse is recommended for optimal experience, but a trackpad can work. Firefox is also the recommended browser. By holding left-click and dragging your mouse across the screen, you can pan around. By using the scroll wheel, you can zoom in and out of the timeline. Objects might appear blurry until you zoom in on them. If you want to focus on a specific event, you can left click that event and it will focus in on it.
